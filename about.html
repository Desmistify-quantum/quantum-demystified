Who We Are

We are passionate physicists dedicated to making quantum mechanics accessible and debunking pseudoscientific claims.

Pedro Mendes

Pedro is an experimental physicist specializing in quantum communication. Currently pursuing a PhD at Instituto Superior Técnico and Instituto de Telecomunicações, his work focuses on developing new quantum communication protocols resistant to daylight noise.

Rafael Carreira

Rafael is a doctoral candidate at Instituto Superior Técnico and Centre of Physics and Engineering of Advanced Materials. His work lies at the intersection of quantum physics and condensed matter, with a focus on exploring quantum materials through theoretical analysis and numerical simulations. Currently, he is investigating lattice gauge theories as a powerful framework for understanding novel quantum phenomena in condensed matter systems.
